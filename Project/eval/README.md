This program is a choose your own adventure where your party enters a cave and passes out from gas and only
you wake up and have to find the cure at the end of the tunnel
there is also a character selection system where everyone has stats that have a role in the game
there is a turn based combat system as well as a sneak/avoid the fight system based on your (Cool/100) which depends on your character selection
there is a mana system where everyone has a special and magic move but its power depends on your class unique to the role
theres an end screen and also a healing system after each room and a string/inventory system available in the safe rooms
